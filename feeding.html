The cats have automatic feeders, so you will not need to be there at their feeding times of 6:00 am and 6:00 pm. The only thing you need to do is make sure the feeders are not empty. This is not difficult, as the feeders have a small window that allows you to see how much food is in them. If they are running low, you can top off their food useing the bad of cat food in the kitchen closet.

If the cats yell at you, it's probably a false alarm. However they may need you to fill up their water dish, and they'll tell you when it's empty. You can make sure it isn't empty by filling it up when you water the plants, that way you won't need to worry about it.

If they're still yelling, they may just want some attention. They're super happy to be petted or even brushed with the furminator on the shelf next to the small plants. If all else fails you can give them some treats. They're in the same closet as the food.
